About JKS Karate Internationl

Founded in 2018 by Sensei Rossy Harinth, JKS Karate International is a rapidly growing and highly respected karate organization with a mission to foster the spirit, discipline, and skill of karate across the globe. Headquartered in Sri Lanka, JKS has established branches in key locations including Jaffna, Mullaitivu, Colombo, Kilinochchi, and even internationally in Australia, bringing our unique approach to karate to diverse communities.

Our Legacy and Achievements

In just a few years, JKS Karate International has made a significant impact, training over 1,000 students worldwide. Our commitment to excellence and personal growth has led our members to succeed at multiple levels. JKS students have won medals and accolades at international tournaments, national championships, provincial competitions, and even inter-university meets, showcasing their dedication and the quality of training provided. Beyond technical skills, JKS emphasizes the values of respect, perseverance, and humility, preparing students not only to be skilled karatekas but also to be responsible and empowered individuals.

Community Impact and Recognition

At JKS Karate International, we believe in using the power of karate to make a positive difference beyond the dojo. We are proud to engage in numerous community service initiatives across Sri Lanka, addressing social challenges, supporting youth empowerment, and promoting mental and physical well-being. Our contributions to society have earned JKS recognition, including a prestigious selection for the United Nations Solidarity Awards for our efforts in building community solidarity and resilience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a global community of karate practitioners who are physically strong, mentally resilient, and socially responsible. JKS Karate International is committed to nurturing each student’s potential, creating leaders in martial arts and beyond. Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced student, JKS offers a supportive environment where you can challenge yourself, achieve new goals, and grow alongside a diverse community.

Join Us

JKS Karate International invites you to join us on a journey of self-discovery, empowerment, and excellence. Experience world-class training, participate in local and international competitions, and be part of an organization that values both tradition and innovation. We are here to help you unlock your potential and make a positive impact on the world.
